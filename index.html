Plan Your Trip

The Berkshires, while beautiful, are not necessarily the easiest place to access. As such, we've provided some resources that might be helpful to ensure that your trip is as pain-free and enjoyable as possible.

Travel

Fly

Bradley International (BDL) - Hartford, CT Albany International (ALB) - Albany, NY

Planes, Trains & Automobiles

While Hartford and Albany are the closest airports to Lenox, we encourage you to take advantage of the multi-modal transportation options afforded by the dense, pre-sprawl urban development of the Northeast. You may be able to find sigificantly cheaper flights to one of New York City's airports. From there, Amtrak offers regular service to Hartford and Springfield, MA.

Lodging

The Berkshires offer a host of lodging options including a variety of hotels, motels, home shares, and B&Bs. No matter your lodging needs, we're confident you'll find a satisfactory roof to shelter you during your trip.

Hampton Inn & Suites

We have reserved a block of rooms at the Hampton Inn & Suites in Lenox. If you would like to receive the reduced block rate, make your reservation by September 15th.
